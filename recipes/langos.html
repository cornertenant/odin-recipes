Lángos

What makes it different

This is no ordinary fried bread.

The Hungarian lángos is puffy yet crispy, makes for a perfect snack!

Ingredients

  • 400 g potatoes
  • 30 g yeast
  • lukewarm milk
  • 20 g sugar
  • 1 kg flour + enough for the forming
  • 25 g salt
  • enough oil to deep fry and to form the dough

For the toppings

  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 400 g sour cream
  • 300 g grated cheese

Steps

  1. cook the potatoes and mash them
  2. bloom the yeast in milk
  3. mix everything together
  4. knead the dough properly
  5. let it rest for at least 45 minutes
  6. form knuckle sized pieces
  7. stetch each piece individually and fry them immidiately (1-2 min each side)
  8. make garlic sauce/paste and apply generously
  9. put on other toppings and enjoy!
