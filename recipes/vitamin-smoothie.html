Vitamin Smoothie

Simple and effective!

This smoothie can be made the previous evening
and it makes the perfect morning drink!

Full of vitamins, nice slightly sour taste
to get your day going.

Ingredients

  • 220 g apples
  • 140 g carrots
  • 100 g beetroot
  • 350 g bananas
  • water

Steps

  1. peel the ingredients
  2. chop them into big chunks
  3. put all in a blender
  4. pour enough water to be 2 cm above
  5. blend it!
  6. serve and enjoy!
